Congratulations!!! Rotaract Club of Ormoc Bay

On August 9, 2020 the Ormoc City Development Office together with the Sangguniang Kabataan conducted a Youth Organization Fair in celebration with the Linggo Ng Kabataan.

Rotaract Ormoc Bay participated the said event together with the different youth organizations in Ormoc City. Blessedly we have won one of the awards, which is the "Most Youthful Booth". Believing that coming together is a beginning, keeping together is a progress, and working together is a SUCCESS.💪 Congratulations ROTARACTORS 🎉 Through the cooperation of every member we made it🥰 . Good job everyone!

Beachurero 2020

On September 19, 2020, the Rotaract of Ormoc Bay joined the yearly International Coastal Clean-up Drive organized by Ormoc City Youth Development Office and the Sangguniang Kabataan- Ormoc City

dubbed as "The Sea Starts with Me" Activity which aims to clean-up the river banks traversing thru the Anilao Bridge, Brgy. Don Felipe Larrazabal Bridge and Brgy. Alegria Bridge. This said activity is in line of the Rotaract's Area of Focus "Supporting the Environment" and the Sustainable Development Goals "Clean Water and Sanitation". This was also participated by various youth organizations in Ormoc City with the special participation of Kiwanis Club of Geo Ormoc and 802nd Infantry Brigade. Indeed, “It is our duty to save the environment’s beauty.”

Orientation.Induction.Election

Earlier today, September 20, 2020 at 1:00-5:00PM, RAC ORMOC BAY had their Orientation for new members and induction of officers and directors for the year 2020-2021 via google meet (Online).

CONGRATULATIONS to President ALEXANDER B. HIPE who will now become the Immediate Past President (IPP) and to our new President ALMIRA I. ANGELIO. It was a success thanks to the special participation of our guest speakers: RCOB. Ma. Lourdes "Apple" Alagon, RCOB. Clerk Jade Aquino, DRR. Ace Justine R. Resare, PDRR. Aiza Marie Moraña, and ADRR. Red Sabela. Of course, this event would not have been successful without the time and effort of IPP, President, other officers, and our new Rotaractors. Indeed, together let us put the ideals of service of rotary- Service above self into realities. "Team will work if there is Teamwork."

Tree Planting Activity
