Recent Activities

Beachurero 2020

On September 19, 2020, the Rotaract of Ormoc Bay joined the yearly International Coastal Clean-up Drive organized by Ormoc City Youth Development Office and the Sangguniang Kabataan- Ormoc City dubbed as "The Sea Starts with Me" Activity which aims to clean-up the river banks traversing thru the Anilao Bridge, Brgy. Don Felipe Larrazabal Bridge and Brgy. Alegria Bridge. This said activity is in line of the Rotaract's Area of Focus "Supporting the Environment" and the Sustainable Development Goals "Clean Water and Sanitation". This was also participated by various youth organizations in Ormoc City with the special participation of Kiwanis Club of Geo Ormoc and 802nd Infantry Brigade. Indeed, “It is our duty to save the environment’s beauty.”

Orientation.Induction.Election

Red Sabela. Of course, this event would not have been successful without the time and effort of IPP, President, other officers, and our new Rotaractors. Cogon Fatima Children's Park Clean-up

On September 05, 2020 despite the threat of COVID-19, Rotaractors still managed to do a simple project and support the sustainable plan of our mother club- Rotary Club Ormoc Bay. In line with one of the Rotary Area of Focus- Supporting the Environment, the Rotaract Ormoc Bay came up with the Project entitled "Cogon Fatima Children's Park Clean-up," which aims to maintain the cleanliness and beauty of the Rotary Park. The environment is where we all meet, what we have in common and what we all share. If we don't want to live in a trash can, then we better stop throwing wastes anywhere.
